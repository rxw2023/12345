About Maillele Water Shoes Factory

Mailele Shoes Factory was established in 2014 and is located in Cixi City, a major shoe manufacturing hub near Shanghai. Our family has been in the shoe-making business for over three decades, and now we focus exclusively on water shoes. These may also be known as beach, swim, surfing, diving, snorkeling, upstream, squat, deadlift, dance or yoga shoes.

We have a team of 37 skilled employees experienced in sole modeling, shoe sewing, and finishing. We have worked with reliable suppliers for materials and digital printing for many years, ensuring quality control from raw materials to the final product.

Our factory has supplied many export companies, with a total export value of around $1,500,000 in 2023 to markets in Asia, the U.S., and the EU. Our domestic sales totaled approximately $500,000.

We have extensive experience in fulfilling both OEM and ODM requests based on customer specifications.
